Delete Your Account

This page explains how you can request that your account and associated data be deleted from our systems.

How to request account deletion

To permanently delete your account for Tassia Central SDA Church, please click the button below and send us a request by email.

Request Account Deletion

What data will be deleted?

When you request deletion:

  • Your account details (name, email, username) will be permanently removed.
  • All associated app usage data, preferences, and content will be deleted.

Retention period: We may retain certain data for up to 30 days if required for legal or security reasons. After this period, it will be permanently deleted.

What we keep: We may retain anonymized usage data that cannot be linked back to you for analytics purposes.

Contact us

If you have questions about your data or the deletion process, please reach out to us at support@lintech_arts.com.
